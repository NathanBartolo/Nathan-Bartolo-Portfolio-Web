RunRight App Design

Project Type App / UX Design
Figma UX Design UI Design Mobile

RunRight is a mobile fitness app designed with a strong focus on intuitive UX and clean, modern visuals. The project covers the full design process from user research and wireframing to high-fidelity prototyping in Figma, creating a seamless and user-centered experience.

The app is designed to motivate runners to stay active by allowing them to track runs, monitor progress, and view performance insights through a clear and engaging interface. Smooth interactions, thoughtful layout decisions, and visually appealing components work together to create an experience that is both functional and motivating.
